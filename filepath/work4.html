Autumn Leaves

Year: 2023

Medium: Oil paint on gessobord

Scale: 16 x 20 inches

In this creation, I found myself enveloped in a moment of serendipitous harmony, a confluence of sound, sight, and spirit. As the iconic jazz notes of "Autumn Leaves" filled my ears, I stood transfixed, a observer in the bustling tapestry of life.
The world around me moved with the rhythm of footsteps. The dance of golden leaves, swirling in the gentle embrace of the breeze outside the window. The sun dapples the room with strokes of light, filtering through the leaves' delicate veins. In this space, the mundane transforms into the magical.
I sought to encapsulate that perfect, fleeting moment, where music, nature, and soul intertwine. Pause, listen, and immerse in the transient yet timeless beauty of autumn, a season that speaks of change, letting go, and the intricate dance of life.
