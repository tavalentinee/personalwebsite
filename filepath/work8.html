Summer Lyricism in Hangzhou

Year: 2023

Medium: Oil paint on canvas

Scale: 12 x 24 inches

In the lingering summer of Hangzhou, where gentle breezes play a tender dance with slanted willow branches, poetry is in motion, a symphony of nature's subtle rhythms. With its soft shimmer, the river mirrors the vibrancy of the grass flourishing under the sunlight.
Half of my heart rejoices in the serene beauty that unfolds before my eyes, while the other half is tinged with a melancholic undertone, dreading the emptiness as the holiday draws close. During these bittersweet intervals, I realized the meaning of "carpe diem," for it is in these fleeting moments that the essence of life truly resides.
