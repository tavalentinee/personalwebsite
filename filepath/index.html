I am not willing to label myself as an 'artist,' as this title feels too heavy for me. I prefer to think of myself as an ordinary person, someone who expresses their inner world through the medium of art. My soul is solitary but not lonely. Words and brushstrokes are the vessels for expressing my emotions.
